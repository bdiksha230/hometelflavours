Our Projects

Weaga Wear Private Limited

Weaga wear is an apparel manufacturing unit. It is a faction for those clothing brands that are budding and want assistance in a smooth establishment. Weaga Wear has a strong research and development network, and its touchpoints are support administration and social expedition.

Gkaa Fashion

Gkaa fashion is a fashion ecosystem that encompasses all aspects of the industry. It's a structure that will take you from a single fiber of your garment to wearing your items in your preferred way. It assists you in exploring the enormous subject of fashion by providing you with content created by Gkaa's designers, stylists, writers, and researchers.

Gkaa Store

Gkaa shop is an online and offline fashion marketplace that sells clothing for men and women of all ages. For the time being, the sort of clothes is 'casual or basic,' but it is anticipated to evolve into several different varieties in the coming years.
